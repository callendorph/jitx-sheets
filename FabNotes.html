Fabrication Notes

  1. TOTAL THICKNESS: NOM 0.062"
    1. USE STANDARD FR4 FOR ALL CORE DIELECTRICS
    2. USE ISOLA 370HR FOR PREPREG DIELECTRICS
  2. USE 1oz COPPER ON ALL LAYERS
  3. APPLY SOLDER MASK OVER BARE COPPER ON BOTH SIDES. SOLDERMASK SHALL BE GREEN IN COLOR
  4. EXPOSED COPPER MUST BE PLATED WITH ELECTROLESS NICKEL IMMERSION GOLD (ENIG)
  5. SILKCREEN LEGEND MUST BE APPLIED OVER SOLDERMASK ON TOP AND BOTTOM. SILKSCREEN SHALL BE WHITE IN COLOR.
  6. THERMAL VIAS UNDER COMPONENTS U2, U19, U22, U23, & U28 MUST BE NON-CONDUCTIVE EPOXY FILLED.

Assembly Notes

  1. WORKMANSHIP TO BE EQUAL TO AND COMPLIANT WITH THE REQUIREMENTS OF IPC-A-610 CLASS II
  2. DEBURR EDGES AFTER DEPANELIZATION.
  3. APPLY URETHANE CONFORMAL COAT EXCEPT WHERE MASKED BY TOP / BOTTOM CONFORMAL COAT MASK GERBERS.
